Welcome to Exercise 2

This exercise will demonstrate the importance of prompt design. A more detailed prompt will usually produce a richer response as opposed to a lazy prompt. Try out a "lazy" query then see how it responds to a more specific prompt. Hint: Try the suggestion buttons!

Exercise 2

Learn how to use LLMs by constructing or "engineering" specific and rich queries.


1. Use the selection of "lazy prompts" and hit send.

2. Study the response. Notice how brief it is.

3. Now, try out a more descriptive query. Ask for something very specific within the topic of interest.

4. Hit send and study the new response. How does it differ? Is it more detailed?
