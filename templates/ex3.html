Exercise 3

Understand the creative limitations of LLMs. Can your creativity improve a standard LLM response?


1. Type in an essay of at least 100 words in the text editor.

2. Generate an essay in this window using the topics in the selection (or choose your own!)

3. Study the response. How does the language and writing style differ between yours and the LLM's?

4. Now, hit send in the text editor. How does the new generation differ?
